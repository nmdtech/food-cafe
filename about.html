About Cherry's Party

Kino presents the best independent food, beers and wines in the heart of Leeds’ cultural scene.

Our Head Chef is former MasterChef: The Professionals semi-finalist Josh Whitehead, whose memorable dishes make the most of the incredible produce available in our region.

Housed in a newly renovated, beautiful Grade II-listed venue, with a long history as an assembly hall, cinema and performance venue, we bring you the best food, beers and wines from Yorkshire producers and local suppliers.

Food is love, and tonight we’re drowning in it!

In today’s world, where everyone is glued to their smartphones and social media, capturing every moment and sharing it with friends and family has become a norm. One such occasion that brings families together and creates memories that are worth sharing is the family dinner.

Be it a fancy restaurant or a cozy home-cooked meal, a family dinner is always special. And what better way to capture these moments than with a perfect caption on Instagram? But, coming up with the right words to express the love, warmth, and joy that a family dinner brings can be challenging.

Food is symbolic of love when words are inadequate.

Food can be very transformational, and it can be more than just about a dish. Watch this video to check how you can enjoy when you're at Cherry's Party.
